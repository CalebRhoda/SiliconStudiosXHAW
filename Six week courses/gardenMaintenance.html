Garden Maintenance Course

Gain essential skills for maintaining a healthy and beautiful garden with our practical Garden Maintenance course.

Benefits

  • Practical Training: Learn hands-on techniques for effective garden care.
  • Certified Training: Receive a certificate upon successful completion.
  • Expert Instructors: Learn from experienced horticulturalists and garden maintenance professionals.

Course Curriculum

  • Module 1: Watering Techniques - Understand the proper watering methods for different types of plants.
  • Module 2: Pruning and Plant Care - Learn how to prune plants and care for their health.
  • Module 3: Soil and Fertilization - Gain knowledge on soil health and how to fertilize for optimal plant growth.

Duration
