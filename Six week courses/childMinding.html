Child Minding Course

Develop essential childcare skills to provide safe and nurturing care for children.

Benefits

  • Practical Experience: Gain hands-on experience in child care.
  • Certified Training: Receive a certificate upon successful completion.
  • Expert Instructors: Learn from professionals with years of childcare experience.

Course Curriculum

  • Module 1: Infant Care - Learn how to care for babies from birth to six months.
  • Module 2: Toddler Development - Understand the developmental needs of toddlers.
  • Module 3: Safety and Nutrition - Learn about child safety, nutrition, and basic first aid.
