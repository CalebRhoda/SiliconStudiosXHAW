First Aid Course

Gain essential skills to handle emergency situations with our comprehensive First Aid training.

Benefits

  • Hands-on Practice: Gain practical experience with real-life simulations.
  • Certified Training: Receive a certificate upon successful completion.
  • Expert Instructors: Learn from professionals with years of experience.

Course Curriculum

  • Module 1: CPR Techniques - Learn CPR methods for adults, children, and infants.
  • Module 2: Emergency Response - Handle common emergencies like choking, bleeding, and burns.
  • Module 3: First Aid Equipment Use - Properly use tools like bandages, splints, and emergency kits.
