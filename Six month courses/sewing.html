Sewing Course

Unlock your creativity and learn essential sewing skills with our comprehensive sewing course designed for beginners and experienced sewists.

Benefits

  • Hands-on Practice: Gain practical experience with various sewing techniques.
  • Certified Training: Receive a certificate upon successful completion of the course.
  • Expert Instructors: Learn from experienced seamstresses and tailors.

Course Curriculum

  • Module 1: Basic Sewing Techniques - Learn essential stitches and sewing machine operation.
  • Module 2: Pattern Making - Understand how to read and create sewing patterns.
  • Module 3: Project Sewing - Complete a sewing project of your choice, such as a garment or home decor item.
