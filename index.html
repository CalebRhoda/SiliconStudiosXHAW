Mission and Vision

To provide high-quality education and skills training that empower individuals to achieve their potential and improve their lives.To create a world where every individual has access to the knowledge and skills necessary to thrive personally and professionally.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We act with honesty and transparency.
  • Respect: We value every individual and foster an inclusive environment.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in all our programs and services.
  • Community: We are committed to making a positive impact in our community.

Our History

Founded in 2018, Empowering the Nation started as a grassroots initiative to address the lack of training opportunities for domestic workers and gardeners. Over the years, we have expanded our offerings to include various courses aimed at improving employability and entrepreneurial skills.
